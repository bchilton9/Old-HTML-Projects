Createing a guild with EQ Guilded

Step 1.

Go into your Edit Settings

Step 2.

Select Create Guild from the menu

Step 3.

Setup a short name for your guild

This will be a username for your guild. It needs to be small with no spaces. For example, If your guilds name is "Guild of All Guild's" make your short name "goag". You can set your guilds full name in the next step.

Step 4.

Enter all the info on your guild

Step 5.

You will then be given a page with the Url to your guilds new webpage.
