Create & Connect

On the start up window press the Button!

This is the Character Database Window!
On the left is the list of your bots.
On the right is the connection info for the bots

Your First step to making a bot is to make an alt.
(If you don't know how to do this don't even try to make a bot).

In the Character Database Window Click the NEW button on the bottom left.

Then on the right fill in the form as follows:

ID:

 bots name
Title: Bots Title (not required)
Host: Furcadias IP
Port: 6000

Click OFFLINE

You will now have a blank black screen
(if you don't you messed something up START OVER)

Now you have to make a trigger.

Click on the Button. You will get the triggers window (did you see that one coming).

This is the Triggers window!
On the left it the triggers You already have
On the right is the triggers properties

The first trigger you have to make is the one that connects your bot to furcadia.

First step to doing this is CLICKING THE NEW BUTTON on the bottom left site.

on the right Fill in the form as follows:

Class: load
Pattern: Press any key to continue
Command: connect AAAA BBBB;
desc CCCC;
color DDDDDDDDDDDDD;

AAAA=Your Bots name EXACTLY how it is in your bots ini file
BBBB=Your Bots password EXALTY how it is in your bots ini file
CCCC=Your bots descripition
DDDDDDDDDDDDD=10 digit color code from your bots ini file
the last 3 will select your bots race/sex/markings
heres the characters and the order

11th character - Gender
[Space] Female
! Male
" Unspecified
12th character - Species
[Space] Rodent
! Equine
" Feline
# Canine
$ Musteline
13th character - Markings
[Space] Plain
! Stripes
" Blotches

$ Rosettes
