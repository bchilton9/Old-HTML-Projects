MUSHclient 101 - Basic Commands

In this tutorial, you'll learn how to do some basic things with your bot in MUSHclient such as
dream entry, movement, actions, and changing maps.

One of the first triggers you'll need for your MUSHclient bot is used for dream entry. Click the
Triggers button, then click the Add button on the triggers window. In the Trigger textbox, type
]q*, then in the Send textbox type vascodagama. Click the OK button and close the Triggers
window. The word vascodagama is the trigger word that allows a character into a dream. So when your bot enters a dream portal, it sends vascodagama to Furcadia and it is sent into the dream.

Now that you've got your bot into a dream, you probably want to move it to a specific location in the dream. To move your bot type one of the following commands and press enter to get the
corresponding effects:

m 1 to move it one space to the southwest
m 3 to move it one space to the southeast
m 7 to move it one space to the northwest
m 9 to move it one space to the northeast

If you want to repeat the same movement command several times you can press the Up key while the text cursor is in the MUSHclient textbox, which makes the last text typed reappear then press enter. If you want your bot to sit, stand, lie down, turn, get, or use type one of the following commands and press enter to make it do the corresponding action:

sit to make the bot sit
stand to make the bot stand
lie to make the bot lie down
< to make the bot turn counterclockwise
> to make the bot turn clockwise
get to make the bot get an object
use to make the bot use an object

Bots are only allowed on Allegria Island and dreams. To move your bot to Allegria Island type
goalleg and press enter.
