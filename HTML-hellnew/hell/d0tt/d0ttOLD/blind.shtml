The police department, famous for its superior K-9 unit, was somewhat taken
aback by a recent incident. Returning home from work, a blonde was shocked
to find her house ransacked and burglarized. She telephoned the police at
once and reported the crime.
The police dispatcher broadcast the call on the channels, and a K-9 unit
patrolling nearby was the first on the scene.
As the K-9 officer approached the house with his dog on a leash, the blonde
ran out on the porch, clapped a hand to her head and moaned, "I come home
from work to find all my possessions stolen, I call the police for help,
and what do they do? They send a BLIND policeman!"
