Create and Connect a FurBot

[1] The first thing you need to do to have a bot with FurBot is create its character information. Open FurBot, click File and go to New Bot. Then click on the "Edit" button towards the bottom of the FurBot window.


[2] A window will popup that has areas to fill in the bots name, password, description, gender, species, and colors.


[3] If you have previously created the character that your bot will be, which is the best thing to do, then you can load the information from that characters INI file. To do this click on the Load From INI button, find the INI file on your computer and click Open. FurBot will fill in the information for you if you load it from an INI file.


[4] If you haven't created the bot character previously then you need to fill out each piece of information. Once you have filled out the information click Save.


[5] A message box will pop up and ask you if you want to register the bot now. If you created the bots character before now then choose No, but if you are creating the bots character now then choose Yes.

If you choose No then another message box will pop up and ask you if you want to register later, choose Yes.










[6] Now that you have created the bots character and filled out the bot information you can connect your bot. Choose Furcadia W3 (default) and click the Connect button. Your bot should connect to Furcadia. Immediately move the bot to your dream or Allegria Island if its not already there.
