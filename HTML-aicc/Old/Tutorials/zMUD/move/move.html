Moving around Furcadia

Moving your bot around furcadia is not an easy thing to do!

All your bot sees is code (If you are already done starting your bot you should see this its the green text)

You will have to follow your bot with your real furry

hears how to make your bot walk

Move commands:

goalleg Allegria Island
gostart Vinca
get Picks up the object its standing over
use uses an object that its holding
who Who is on the map
lie Lie down
< Turns clockwise
> Turns counter-clockwise
m 1 moves southwest
m 3 moves south east
m 7 moves northwest
m 9 moves northeast
wh Name Message Whispers Message to Name
"Message Says Message
