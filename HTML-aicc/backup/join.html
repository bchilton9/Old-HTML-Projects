AICC Membership Requirements

Botmaking Experience Anyone wanting to become a member of the AICC should have extended knowledge with at least one of the botmaking clients the AICC helps furres to learn I.E. FurBot, zMUD, MUSHclient, and/or Visual Basic.
Proper Grammar Usage When a member is helping an aspiring botmaker, they should use proper grammar at all times to be sure the furre being helped can properly understand what is being explained.
Referral From A Current Member A current AICC member should review your botmaking skills, and if they believe you are experienced enough to be a member, then they will refer you to the Rah.
Interview By Red Dragon After being referred by a current member of the AICC, Red Dragon will interview you. If accepted, you should have already chosen a faction to participate in and put [AICC - FactionNameHere] in your character's description.
